\section{Conclusion}
ContextTrace represents RAG failures as inspectable chains connecting answer
claims, evidence spans, citations, source conditions, abstention, root causes,
and repairs. Its deterministic pipeline, production-path ablations, same-ID
baseline handling, and fail-closed evidence gates make the evaluation auditable.
Frozen pre-review results are promising, but model-review sensitivity and the
negative simulated actionability result reinforce the need for independent
human validation. The next empirical milestone is completion of the blinded
RAGTruth, Diag-150, and three-reviewer RQ4 protocols.
