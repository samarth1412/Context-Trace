# Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund within 30 calendar days of the purchase date when the product is unused or the service has not been substantially consumed. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method.

Refund approval normally takes two business days after support receives the required details. Payment processing can take five to seven business days after approval, depending on the bank or card network.

Final sale items, gift cards, and accounts closed for policy abuse are not eligible for refunds. Damaged items can be reviewed when the customer provides a photo and proof of purchase.
